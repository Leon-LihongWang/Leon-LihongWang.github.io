A 1st-year Master's degree candidate in Computer Science. Where there is a will, there is a way

I am a first-year Master’s degree candidate in the Intelligent Content Learning (ICL) Group at Jilin University, under the supervision of Associate Professor Rui Ma, who leads the group, and Professor Tieru Wu, who serves as the group mentor.

My fascination with computer vision began with my initial encounter with the SIFT operator. Its elegance and complementary operational modes made me appreciate the manner in which computers comprehend the world through images.
